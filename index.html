Delete Account

If you wish to permanently delete your AIM Education account and associated data, follow the instructions below.

Steps

  1. Open AIM Education App.
  2. Login using your Google Account.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your request.

What will be deleted?

  • Your account
  • Your profile
  • Your learning progress
  • Your quiz history
  • Your bookmarks
